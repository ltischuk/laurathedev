About Me

Hello! 👋

My name is Laura and I'm a passionate software developer, architect & leader. I have 15+ years of software development experience and currently lead high-performing engineering teams that create flagship e-Commerce software processing over $2B in transaction volume globally. If you recently bought a ticket to a theme park, museum, or other attraction, chances are you have used the apps I build.

I treat every product I ship as my own. I work relentlessly with stakeholders to understand problems and strategy from all perspectives — stepping outside the role of "just an engineer" to dive deep into market research, understand the competitive landscape, and identify the emerging trends that should drive engineering focus.

Today's engineering landscape is changing at an extraordinary pace. I have been actively following and applying the latest in AI engineering pipelines — spec-driven development, harness design for optimum AI output, and strong architectural principles that allow teams to deliver with speed without compromising quality or performance. My current go-to AI thought-partner tools are Claude Code and Opencode, and I have experimented extensively with building custom AI assistance using the Hermes agent framework. That said, I believe now more than ever that a solid foundation in security, reliability, and scalability is what separates great systems from merely fast ones.
